PURCHASE SEASONING BOILING EATING
1.
PURCHASE
Buy one or multiple instant noodles in convenient stores or surpermarkets!
*Recommend the brand CUP NOODLES!
SHOW
2.
SEASONING
Open the package and insert the seasoning powders. Note that some seasoning packs are to be inserted after process 3.
3.
BOILING
Use a water boiler to boil enough amount of water for cooking to noodles. You could also use a microwave instead. Close the lid of the noodle cup and wait for 3-5 minutes depending the type of the noodle.
4.
EATING
Insert seasoning packs that should be inserted at this point if there is any. Stir the noodles to mix all the ingredients completely, prepare a pair of chopsticks or fork or whatever and you have yourself a delicious instant noodle prepared!
